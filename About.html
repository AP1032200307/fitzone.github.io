24 HOUR FITNESS

Located in the industrial zone of Andheri "24 HOUR FITNESS" was started in 2012. We take the opportunity to introduce ourselves as one of the leading manufacturers and importers of hi-tech fitness products in the brand name of "BODY GYM". We are one of the top indoor and outdoor fitness equipment manufacturing companies in India, known for unmatched product quality and customer support.

Body Gym Fitness equipment was founded in 1996, in Mumbai. Initially we started with manufacturing and import of domestic fitness equipment, today we have a large range of fitness equipment, both indoor and outdoor, including exercise bikes, home gyms, cross trainers, benches, and many more. We serve all major cities of India with utmost customer value and continuous support.

Not just bikes and benches, we have also extended our production lines to making the Outdoor Fitness Equipment. A whole range of fitness equipment known by the names of outdoor fitness, green gym, park fitness etc…, setup in parks and open grounds is a great idea to promote fitness among families and different age groups. Also being beneficial and cost effective, these machines are not only meant for teenagers or adults but for an age group of 10 year – 90 years, which includes children, woman and senior citizen too.
